Project overview

  • This is a financial dashboard that simulates a bank's clients. We model companies, individuals, and their cash and stock transactions. We built a web platform where users can view, search, and analyze their financial data.

Users

  • Admin: Can browse all views, search lists of companies and individuals, and drill into every company/individual dashboard. Has access to a global view of transactions and stock holdings.
  • Companies: Get a dashboard with an overview of accounts and transactions for a selected period. They see income, expenses, profit/loss, and payroll.
  • Individual account holders: Get a personal dashboard with bank accounts, brokerage holdings, and an overview of income and expenses by category.

Scope and functionality

  • Server-rendered dashboards: All dashboards are rendered server-side with Jinja2 templates. We use SQLAlchemy ORM queries to populate them.
  • Search and filtering: Admins can search and filter across all data.
  • Dynamic dashboards: The ORM layer is structured so dashboards and charts can be built dynamically. More on this later.
  • Easy setup: The entire project can be built in one go with make quickstart.
