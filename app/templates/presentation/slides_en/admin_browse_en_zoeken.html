Admin browse and search

Dashboard overview

The admin dashboard aggregates data across all individuals, companies, and their transactions.

  • Four main views: Individuals, Companies, Transactions, and Assets under management.
  • Clicking a card loads the corresponding view.

Search and filtering

Each list in the admin dashboard supports search and sorting. Admins can query in realtime and sort columns.

Views for individuals and companies

Overviews of all individuals and companies in the platform, with links into each dashboard.

  • Individuals: Name, job title, employer, monthly income, account balances, and brokerage assets.
  • Companies: Company name, employee count, profit margin, and other metrics.

Transactions and holdings

Admins can also browse transactions and stock holdings.

  • Transactions overview: List of recent transactions.
  • Stock holdings: Overview of all investment portfolios with real market price data.
