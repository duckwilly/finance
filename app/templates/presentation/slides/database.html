Database design

Schema built for financial data: parties, accounts, double-entry journals, and stock holdings with historical prices.

Principles

  • Separation of parties (companies vs individuals) with searchable admin listings.
  • Ledger-first view of transactions so totals balance and drill-downs stay consistent.
  • Stock prices and FX rates fetched historically to keep performance demos grounded in real signals.

Talking points

  • Schema SQL lives in sql/schema.sql and backs the seed CSVs for predictable demo data.
  • Session helpers in app/db keep connections scoped per request.
  • Admin dashboards precompute metrics on startup so the first slide feels instant.
