Project overview

A FastAPI-driven finance platform that simulates bank clients, models companies and individuals, and renders rich admin dashboards.

  • Audience: admins, business owners, and individual account holders navigating the same data with scoped access.
  • Scope: server-rendered dashboards, AI-assisted insights, and drill-downs into companies, individuals, transactions, and holdings.
  • Philosophy: keep business logic modular and the UI responsive so we can demo data stories without leaving the browser.
