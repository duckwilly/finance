Database

Nature of our data

Seeded like a real bank: 552 parties (501 individuals, 51 companies), 1,553 accounts, 101,941 journal entries with 203,882 lines, and 4,226 trades priced by 7,405 quotes.

What we capture

  • Parties with separate individual/company profiles plus memberships, employment contracts, and access grants.
  • Cash and brokerage accounts in EUR/USD/GBP with typed roles for owners, managers, and viewers.
  • Double-entry journals with categories and sections (income, expense, transfer) for clean P&L slices.
  • Instruments, trades, holdings, and daily price quotes to back portfolio and unrealized P&L views.
  • Reporting facts (cash flow, payroll, holding performance) pre-aggregated per period for dashboards.

Concrete examples

  • Payroll run T-0000001 credits A-U00128-CHK (Vince Boetet) and balances against A-GL-INCOME.
  • Transfer T-0000002 mirrors that payroll from Canterbury Architects LLC (C-00028) as a company expense.
  • Trade TR-HIST-0000001: A-U00270-BRK buys 2.70 NVDA at 334.36 USD with recorded fees and tax for cost basis.
  • Price quotes anchor holdings: 7,405 rows across NVDA/MSFT/VWRL and more, keyed by instrument + date.
