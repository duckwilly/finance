Demo flow

Features & walkthrough

A modular story: pivot across companies, individuals, transactions, and holdings without leaving the SPA.

  • Kick off from the admin dashboard: cards swap panels via HTMX to explore people, companies, transactions, and stocks.
  • Charts are prepared server-side (Chart.js friendly data) so every swap immediately updates visuals and lists together.
  • Use the navigation rail to jump to key stops: company drill-downs, individual cash flow, or stock leaderboards.
  • Demo script idea: show a company trending down, open its ledger, then pull AI charts to narrate what changed.
